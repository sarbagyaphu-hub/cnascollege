About CNAS

A Multi-Level Institution Built for Academic Growth

CNAS College is envisioned as a modern academic institution where students can pursue nationally recognized education, practical training, and professional development in one integrated learning environment. The institution combines high school studies, university-level education, and industry-oriented diploma training so learners can grow from foundation to specialization.

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, relevant, and high-quality education that strengthens academic capability, employability, and character through innovation, discipline, and opportunity.

Our Vision

To become a respected center of academic excellence in Kathmandu by connecting education with leadership, skills, global standards, and future-oriented programs.

Our Promise

At CNAS, students are guided through supportive teaching, modern learning methods, practical exposure, and a campus culture that values growth, confidence, and achievement.

Bachelor's Programs

Affiliated with Tribhuvan University (TU)

BBS — Bachelor of Business Studies

BBS is designed to develop students into capable managers and professionals with strong understanding of business principles, organization, finance, accounting, and practical decision making. It is ideal for students who want broad management grounding.

BBA — Bachelor of Business Administration

BBA focuses on modern management, leadership, entrepreneurship, organizational behavior, communication, and professional readiness. It suits students seeking dynamic preparation for corporate, administrative, and entrepreneurial careers.
